The Hunt

An invitation, to puzzlers, hunters, and adventurers.

You’re invited to hunt through the mythologies and rhymes of the City of London, in search of the heart of the city.

Rumours abound that this hunt has been called by a dragon, newly in human form.

That perhaps you’ll face this dragon at the end of the hunt, and answer its riddle.

And to the winner, a prize of a golden key.

How do I know this?

Because strange as it appears, even to me - I am this dragon.

And you can call me Clessa

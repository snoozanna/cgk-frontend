The Hunt

For puzzlers, code-breakers and seekers of hidden treasure, The Hunt is a treasure hunt: for real.

It is called by a dragon, Clessa, for the Heart of the City.

The Heart of the City has always changed, and changes again on the 15th October. Clessa is trying to find the new (human) custodian of the Heart of the City, and award the new custodian the golden key.

In the lead up to the 15th October Clessa will publish online the pages of a book: London's Assorted Mythologies And Enigmas In Rhyme. This book contains everything you need to solve in order to find the place and the shape of the new Heart of the City, and where to start the Hunt on the day.

The Hunt leads you with the aid of your smartphone across key places in the mythology of the Heart of the City, telling you its story and of its custodians. At the end, you'll find yourself presented with a map where you make your best guess to the place of the Heart. Everyone can make a guess to place the Heart, but if you've solved all the cryptic clues inside the book you'll know exactly where...

Everyone who then has placed the Heart - right or wrong - will then receive an invitation for a ceremony happening at the end of the day in a top-secret location. There you'll meet Clessa, and have a chance to answer her final riddle: about the Heart itself.

Whoever answers the riddle best, wins the golden key.

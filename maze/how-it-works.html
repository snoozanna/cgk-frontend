The Maze - How it works

Some routes welcome families, some have age restrictions. Some routes have step-free access but a handful of routes end up in historical buildings that are not accessible to wheelchair users. Explore the map to find out a route that suits you.

Go to the location indicated on the map: on those public squares and gardens, you will find a red, wooden door, with a door keeper showing the key to start the adventure.

You will need a smartphone - either per person or per group - to lead you, with access to mobile data.

The door keeper will invite you to scan a QR code or send a text message to open the adventure online. You will not need to download any app. Normal messaging costs and data will apply.

You will then be guided in audio and captions, so bring headphones if you like, but each adventure can be enjoyed without.

You can expect each adventure, including the end-surprise, to take no more than 1 hour.

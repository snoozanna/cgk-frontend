The Golden Key

How to get there

The Golden Key will take place in 15 different locations across the City of London. Please check our interactive map below to get an overview of all locations, or refer to each individual event page to find how to get to the event of your choice.

Too much information? The easiest access point are the three Fire Fairs: there will be information points directing you to other parts of the event, including ways to start the Maze and the Hunt.

The Fair of Pathways and Passage

@ St Paul's Churchyard, London EC4M 8AD

Nearest stations: St Paul’s Underground station, City Thameslink station and Blackfriars Underground and train station

The Fair of Topsy Turvy Power

@ Guildhall, Gresham Street, London EC2V 7HH

Nearest stations: Bank, Mansion House, St. Pauls, Moorgate, Cannon Street and Liverpool Street

The Fair of Tall and Small Stories

@ Museum of London & the London Wall Highwalks, 150 London Wall, London EC2Y 5HN

Nearest stations: Barbican, St Paul’s, Liverpool Street, Farringdon, Cannon Street

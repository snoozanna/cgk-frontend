The Golden Key

How to get there

The Golden Key will take place in 15 different locations across the City of London. Please check our interactive map below to get an overview of all locations, or refer to each individual event page to find how to get to the event of your choice.

Too much information? The easiest access point are the three Fire Fairs: there will be information points directing you to other parts of the event, including ways to start the Maze and the Hunt.

The Fair of Pathways and Passage

  • By train

    It couldn’t be easier to visit the City by train with Southeastern, so you can sit back and relax as you travel into the heart of London. Southeastern offer a variety of ticket options and fares, plus there are no booking fees when you book direct with Southeastern.

    Advance Southeastern offer Advance tickets which are available on selected routes as single tickets at specific times and a great way to save on train travel. Book early for cheaper fares.

    Kids for a Quid An adult with a valid ticket can travel with up to 4 kids (aged 5-15) for just £1 each on off-peak Southeastern services, all year round. Under 5s go free.

    GroupSave Save up to 34% on the price of individual adult tickets when groups of 3-9 adults travel together off-peak. Plus, any kids travelling in addition to the group of adults will be eligible for Kids for a Quid tickets.

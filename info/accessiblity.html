Accessibility

We do our best to make our events accessible and inclusive. All shows and sites are relaxed and we invite audiences to feel at home at our events. Please contact our team of event staff and volunteers who will be happy to support you with specific access requirements.

The sites at St Paul’s Cathedral’s Fair, Guildhall Fairand London Wall Fair:

  • Are wheelchair accessible

  • Are assistance dog friendly

  • Have chairs available

  • Have accessible toilets

  • Have a programme accessible for audiences of all ages



The Maze of Adventures and The Hunt include 13 different walking routes with different access features:

  • All routes involve walking time ranging from 10 to 30 minutes. Please refer to each individual route page for additional information.

  • 10 routes are fully accessible to wheelchair users, but 3 routes are finishing in historical buildings with no step-free access. Please check the tags on the Maze map to find out which routes include step-free access.

  • The audio sections of all Maze routes and the Hunt include integrated captions: please refer to each individual adventure for additional details about the Maze ‘surprises’, including the routes leading to speech free activities and the availability of transcripts.

Please contact us on destination.city@cityoflondon.gov.uk for further information about accessibility and we will endeavour to assist.

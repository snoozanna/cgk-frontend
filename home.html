The Golden Key

The Golden Key is a spectacular free event transforming the City of London into a large-scale programme of immersive performance and participatory events on Saturday 15th October 2022.

Inspired by the history and hidden tales of the Square Mile, audiences are guided through the City’s streets on an array of magical experiences by a host of characters conjured up from the past and future.

Practical Details

Event highlights include three Fire Fairs, colourful family-friendly festivals of performance in Guildhall, alongside the London Wall Highwalks and around St Paul’s Cathedral; the Maze of Adventures, twelve smartphone-led adventures leading to immersive experiences in some of the City’s best-kept secret locations; and The Hunt: a secret quest to find the Heart of the City, and a treasure hunt, for real.

The Golden Key is brought to you by Coney & Friends and the City of London Corporation.

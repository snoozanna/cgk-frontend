The Golden Key

The Golden Key is a spectacular event transforming the City of London into a large-scale programme of immersive performance and participatory events.

Inspired by the secrets of the Square Mile, this spectacular free event will transform the City of London’s streets with theatre, games and performance for all ages.

Featuring 200+ performers, three large-scale street festivals, a mass treasure hunt, a ‘maze’ of immersive adventures and creative street theatre, you’ll be guided by a host of characters conjured up from the past and future.
